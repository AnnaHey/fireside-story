It was a dark and stormy night...

A flash of lightning revealed a rundown shack silhouetted against the top of the hill.

As the rain pelted me in the face, a strange sense of confidence overcame me and I walked towards the rundown shack.

The wind blew my umbrella inside out, and I tossed it away. Where I was going, an umbrella wasn't going to help anyway.

I walked towards the big maple tree, barely seeing my way, and suddenly, I heard a strange noise, was that a frog?
